Our Mission

We want to make it easier to help.

At Voitheia, our main mission is in giving a voice to the voiceless.

Too often, donations with charitable intention are funneled away, leaving a fraction left for those it was meant to go to. At Votheia, we avoid that. All money goes straight to whom it was intended, no middlemen involved.

Access is key to easy charity. All transactions are performed through hassle-free payment gateways, that support most methods of payment.

Voitheia strives to serve as an autonomous, intuitive and simplified means to charity.

FAQ

frequently asked questions

What kind of causes are on this site?

Explcility, any cause that helps people in some direct manner has a place in Voitheia. This can mean anything from government schools to underfunded research projects to environmental programs, and everything in between.

What are the methods of payment? How do I give money to a particular cause?

We use multiple payment gateways including the Instamojo© and Danamojo© services to provide a wide variety of payment methods, including bank transfers, debit cards, credit cards, e-payments, with 256 bit SSL security. This money is sent directly to their appropriate accounts.

Does my entire amount go straight to the desired profile? Am I charged for performing the transaction?

For pages using Instamojo©, 2% of the value of the transaction + ₹3 is the transaction fee. Danamojo© does not have any fees. We ourselves do not take any money from the transaction.

I know of or run a social cause that would be relevant for this site. Can it be added?

You can go ahead and send us the details! After approval, you can set up yout page and start receiving donations!
